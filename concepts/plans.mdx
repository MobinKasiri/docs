## Overage Policy

How NexoraNode handles requests beyond your monthly quota depends on your plan:

- **Free plan** — Requests are **blocked** once you reach your 1,000,000 monthly limit. Your endpoint returns a `429 Too Many Requests` error until your quota resets at the start of the next billing cycle. To avoid interruption, upgrade to Pro.

- **Pro plan** — Requests beyond your 25,000,000 monthly allotment are charged at **$0.0000015 per request** (i.e., $1.50 per 1,000,000 additional requests). Overage billing appears on your next invoice. You can set a spending cap in the dashboard to prevent unexpected charges.

- **Enterprise plan** — Requests are **unlimited**. There are no hard caps and no overage charges — your rate limit and infrastructure capacity are negotiated as part of your contract.

<Note>
  Overage pricing is subject to change. Always refer to [nexoranode.xyz/pricing](https://nexoranode.xyz/pricing) for the current rates before building billing assumptions into your application.
</Note>

## Upgrading Your Plan

Upgrading takes effect **immediately** — you gain access to higher rate limits and new features the moment the upgrade is processed.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Dashboard">
    Log in to your account at [app.nexoranode.xyz](https://app.nexoranode.xyz).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Billing">
    In the left sidebar, click **Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Plan">
    Click **Upgrade** next to the plan you want, review the pricing summary, and confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Using Your New Limits">
    Your new rate limits and features are active immediately. No endpoint URL changes are required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To inquire about an **Enterprise contract**, click **Contact Sales** on the Billing page or email [sales@nexoranode.xyz](mailto:sales@nexoranode.xyz).

</Note>
